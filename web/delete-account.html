Delete your account and data

Last updated: [DATE]

You can delete your Kumo account and everything stored under it, at any time, for any reason. This page explains what gets removed and how to do it — including a way that does not require installing the app.

What deletion removes

  • Your creature and its progress (level, evolution, badges)
  • Your habits and their history
  • Every completion event and daily summary
  • Your anonymous account record itself

Health and usage data is never stored on our servers, so there is nothing of that kind to delete on our side. Removing the app's permissions in your device settings stops any further reading of it.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Kumo and go to the You tab.
  2. Choose Delete my account.
  3. Confirm. Your local data is cleared and your cloud record is removed.

Request deletion without the app

Because Kumo accounts are anonymous, we cannot look you up by name or email. To have your data deleted, email [CONTACT EMAIL] from your device and include your account identifier, which you can find in the app under You → About. We will confirm and complete the deletion within [30] days.

Uninstalling

Uninstalling the app removes the copy of your data held on that device. Data already synced to the cloud is removed only by one of the deletion methods above.
