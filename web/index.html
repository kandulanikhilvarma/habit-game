Android · early access

The app where using Instagram less makes something you love grow.

Kumo reads your screen time and your health data on your phone, and turns the habits you actually keep into a creature that grows with you. No logging. No guilt. No streak you have to fake.

  • Zero taps. Steps, sleep and screen time verify themselves. The game moves because your life moved.
  • A creature, not a checklist. It gets sleepy, not angry. Miss a day and it waits for you.
  • Your data stays yours. Health and usage data is read on your device. Only the completion events sync.

Closed beta opens at Gate 1. Ask for an invite.
